AI as a trend shaping the future of work

The 2023 Future of Jobs Report is based on a survey of 803 of the world's largest employers, representing 11.3 million workers in 27 industry clusters and 45 economies. The report covers macrotrends, technology trends, their impact on jobs and skills, and workforce transformation strategies.

Businesses expect AI and adoption of new and frontier technologies to have a significant impact on their operations, with more than 86% of respondents saying it will drive transformation in their organization.

AI and macrotrends are expected to have a significant impact on jobs in sectors such as healthcare, manufacturing, and finance.
